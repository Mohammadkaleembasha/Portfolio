PPE DETECTION

Description:

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) detection is a process that involves identifying whether individuals are wearing the required safety gear in specific environments, particularly in workplaces like construction sites, factories, laboratories, and healthcare settings. PPE includes items such as helmets, safety vests, gloves, goggles, face masks, and ear protection

Technologies Used:

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Deep learning, YOLO, OpenCV.
